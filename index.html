Today's topic is "cultural appropriation." Cultural appropriation, at times also phrased cultural misappropriation, is the adoption of an element or elements of one culture by members of another culture. The article talks about beauty of black female and hair braids. While white female feel confused when they were criticized for wearing braids or trying to look exotic with makeup and styles as it could offend the minority group who originated those culture, such as braids came from black slaves' working on cornfield. The author criticize Kim Kardashian for making profit from branding and perfecting black beauty while the black female sometimes feel frustrated dealing with their natural afro hair.
